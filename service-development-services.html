User Interface (UI) Design

The user interface (UI) serves as the focal point for human-computer interaction and communication within a device. This encompasses various elements such as display screens, keyboards, mice, and the visual layout of a desktop. It serves as the conduit through which users engage with applications or websites.

With the rising reliance of numerous businesses on web and mobile applications, many companies are now placing greater emphasis on UI design to enhance the overall user experience.

User Experience (UX) Design

User experience (UX) design is the systematic approach that design teams employ to develop products that deliver meaningful and pertinent experiences to users. It encompasses the entire process of acquiring and integrating the product, incorporating elements such as branding, design, usability, and functionality.
